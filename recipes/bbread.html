Bannana Bread recipe

This page will teach you how to make a Banana bread recipe from scratch

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 2 medium bannanas, sliced

Directions:

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees (175 celsius) Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan
  2. Stir sugar and melted butter togther in a large bowl. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter until smooth
  3. Fold in banana slices, sour cream, walnuts; transfer into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean, about 1 hour.
  5. Cool loaf in the pan for 10 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
