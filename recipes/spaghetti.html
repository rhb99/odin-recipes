Homemade Spaghetti & Ground Beef

This page will teach you how to make homemade Spaghetti with ground beef

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps to make Homemade Spaghetti & Ground Beef

  1. Gather and seperate all ingrediants
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  4. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti suace for 1 hour stirring ocassionally.
  5. Stir Sauce into cooked noodles, Serve hot and enjoy!
