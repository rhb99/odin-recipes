The Perfect Basic Burger

This page will teach you how to create a basic burger recipe you are guarenteed to love!

Ingrediants:

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps to create the perfect Basic Burger

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill to high heat, lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk together egg, salt and pepper in a medium sized bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until blended.
  4. Form in to 3/4 inch-thick patties
  5. Place patties on preheated grill. Cover and cook for 6-8 minutes per side, if using a thermometor aim for 160 degrees F (70 degrees celsius)
  6. Serve while hot, add desired toppings and enjoy!
